REVIEW

David Kong October 2025

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – The Movie: Infinity Castle is everything an anime adaptation should be. Everything is perfectly executed, bringing the original material to life and elevating it to cinematic greatness. The storytelling is masterful, with every scene crafted to draw you deeper into the world.

The performances are outstanding, giving depth and emotion to each character. Even at more than two hours long, the pacing never drags. It’s visually stunning, emotionally gripping, and leaves you thinking long after the credits roll.

An absolute must-watch for any Demon Slayer fan — a film that sets a new standard for anime movies.

Trivia

  • The film made ¥1.64 billion (~US $11.1 million) on opening day in Japan (July 18 2025), setting a new record for the highest first-day gross in Japanese cinema history.
  • It will be part of a planned trilogy of Demon Slayer films.
  • Grossed $71 million domestically in its opening weekend — the most of any anime film in U.S. history, surpassing Pokémon: The First Movie (1998).
