Bio

Hi! I’m David Kong. I’m a cybersecurity student at New England Institute of Technology, pursuing a career that’s both challenging and low impact after spending 12 years in the United States Air Force working in Electrical and Environmental Systems. I was also employed at General Dynamics Electric Boat where I’ve worked for over 15 years as a shipfitter supporting submarine construction. Outside of work and school, I enjoy fishing, weightlifting, boating, and hiking. I’m also a fan of tabletop games like Magic: The Gathering and Dungeons & Dragons, and I love spending downtime cooking and trying out new recipes. I’m excited to be here and look forward to learning more about cybersecurity and web development!
